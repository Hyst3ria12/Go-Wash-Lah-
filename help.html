WHAT IS THE TURNAROUND TIME?

We understand the importance of a quick turnaround time to accommodate our customers' busy schedules. Once your laundry is in our hands, we strive to return it to you as promptly as possible without compromising on quality. Our standard turnaround time typically ranges from 24 to 48 hours, depending on the volume and type of items being laundered. We work diligently to ensure that your garments are thoroughly cleaned and meticulously inspected before being ready for pickup. In cases where you require expedited service, we also offer same-day and next-day options for a nominal fee.
